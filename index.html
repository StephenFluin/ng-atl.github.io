What is ngAtl?

Founded in 2017 ngAtlanta is a conference with two focuses:

  1. Diversity and Inclusion
  2. Angular
One of the goals for this conference when it was originally conceived was to have at least 50% of the speakers be Women in Technology. This is something we are taking quite seriously. As such we are happy to announce that currently our speaker list is sitting at 85% women in technology!


While we are preparing our official site please take a few moments to sign up for our mailing list.

As we have updates and announce speakers we will keep you updated.
