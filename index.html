What is ngAtl?

Founded in 2017 ngAtlanta is a conference with two focuses:
  1. Diversity and Inclusion (see our Code of Conduct)
  2. Angular
One of our initial goals was to have a diverse speaker lineup with at least 50% of our speakers Women in Tech.

Currently ngAtl is sitting at 91% diversity speakers of which 82% women in tech.

While we are preparing our official site please take a few moments to sign up for our mailing list.

As we have updates and announce speakers we will keep you updated.
